About Us

"A diverse mix of voices leads to better discussions, decisions, and outcomes for everyone." ~ Sundar Pichai

We proudly present to you "LangVox", your one and only stop for synthesized speech for the underrepresented languages and dialect

Our proposed innovation involves the development of a generative model that creates synthetic speech samples for underrepresented languages and dialects, with the aim of promoting linguistic diversity and reducing language barriers in speech technology.

This generative model will have the capability to produce speech samples in a wide range of languages and dialects, particularly those that are currently underrepresented in speech datasets. The model will utilize cutting-edge machine learning techniques to create high-quality synthetic speech samples that are natural-sounding and indistinguishable from human speech.

Our Future Scope:

Linguistic Diversity

The use of this generative model will promote greater linguistic diversity and inclusivity by supporting the development of speech and text recognition models for a broader range of languages and dialects.
This, in turn, will facilitate communication across different cultures and promote greater understanding and collaboration among people from diverse linguistic backgrounds.

Digital Archive

This innovation will help preserve and promote endangered languages and dialects by creating a digital archive of synthetic speech and text samples.
This archive will serve as a valuable resource for linguistic research, education, and documentation, and may even help to revive some endangered languages.

Advancement

The success of this innovation could inspire further research and development in the field of language technology, leading to the creation of new and more advanced models for speech and text generation.
This, in turn, could help overcome the challenges of language barriers and support greater linguistic diversity and inclusivity in the global community.
